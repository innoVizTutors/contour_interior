Our Foundation

Trust & Craft

Our contracting firm was founded by Mr. Parag Edwankar, B.E. (Civil), who carries forward a rich legacy of construction and architecture ingrained in his family across three generations.

Contours Interio specialises in providing turnkey interior design and execution services for both residential and commercial spaces, including hotels. With comprehensive solutions encompassing civil, electrical, carpentry, painting, and plumbing works, we are a single point of accountability for every project.
