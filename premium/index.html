Our Foundation

A Legacy Built on

Our contracting firm was founded by Mr. Parag Edwankar, B.E. (Civil), who carries forward a rich legacy of construction and architecture ingrained in his family. Guided by this heritage, Contours Interio carries the belief that the spaces we inhabit shape our experiences, influence our emotions and tell our stories.

With over 3 decades of experience, whether it's creating elegant residential spaces or sophisticated commercial environments — we blend traditional expertise with modern innovation to craft environments that reflect the unique personalities of those who occupy them.
